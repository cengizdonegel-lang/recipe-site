Menemen

Traditional Turkish egg dish with tomatoes and peppers

Menemen Recipe
Description

Menemen is a traditional Turkish breakfast dish made with eggs, tomatoes, green peppers, and spices. It's simple, flavorful, and perfect for starting your day.

Ingredients
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1 green pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
Instructions
  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced green peppers and onions, saut√© until softened.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes and cook until they break down and release their juices.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  5. Crack the eggs directly into the pan and gently stir to combine with the vegetables.
  6. Cook until eggs reach your desired consistency.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot with bread.
Chef's Tip: For a creamier texture, don't overcook the eggs. Some prefer it with large egg curds, while others like it more scrambled - it's all about personal preference!
