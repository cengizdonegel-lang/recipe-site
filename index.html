Menemen Recipe
Description

Menemen is a traditional Turkish breakfast dish made with eggs, tomatoes, green peppers, and spices. It's simple, flavorful, and perfect for starting your day.

Ingredients
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1 green pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
Instructions
  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced green peppers and onions, sauté until softened.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes and cook until they break down and release their juices.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  5. Crack the eggs directly into the pan and gently stir to combine with the vegetables.
  6. Cook until eggs reach your desired consistency.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot with bread.
Mediterranean Salad Recipe
Description

A refreshing Mediterranean salad packed with fresh vegetables, feta cheese, and olives. Perfect for a light lunch or as a side dish.

Ingredients
  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup black olives
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh oregano
Instructions
  1. Wash and prepare all vegetables.
  2. In a large bowl, combine greens, cucumber, tomatoes, and onion.
  3. Add feta cheese and olives.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour dressing over salad and toss gently.
  6. Garnish with fresh oregano and serve immediately.
Chocolate Lava Cake Recipe
Description

A decadent chocolate dessert with a molten center. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream for the ultimate indulgence.

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 4 oz dark chocolate
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter and cocoa for ramekins
  • Vanilla ice cream (optional)
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Butter and dust 4 ramekins with cocoa powder.
  3. Melt butter and chocolate together in a double boiler.
  4. In a bowl, whisk eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until thick.
  5. Fold melted chocolate into egg mixture.
  6. Gently fold in flour and salt.
  7. Divide batter among ramekins.
  8. Bake for 12-14 minutes until edges are firm but center is soft.
  9. Let cool for 1 minute, then invert onto plates.
  10. Serve immediately with ice cream.
Stir-Fried Noodles Recipe
Description

Quick and delicious stir-fried noodles with vegetables and your choice of protein. Ready in under 15 minutes!

Ingredients
  • 8 oz noodles (rice or egg noodles)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, broccoli)
  • 1 cup protein of choice (chicken, shrimp, or tofu)
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Green onions for garnish
  • Sesame seeds (optional)
Instructions
  1. Cook noodles according to package directions, drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat.
  3. Add garlic and stir-fry for 30 seconds.
  4. Add protein and cook until done, about 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add cooked noodles to the wok.
  7. Pour in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil.
  8. Toss everything together for 1-2 minutes.
  9. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
